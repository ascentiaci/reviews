I am very pleased to share my positive experience at Elevare College. The staff are highly educated, cooperative, and friendly, always ready to support students whenever needed. Their approachable nature creates a welcoming atmosphere that makes learning comfortable and enjoyable. The college environment is excellent—clean, safe, and well-maintained—which helped me stay focused and motivated throughout my studies.
I completed my HCA course classes here and am now ready to begin my practicum. Throughout my entire journey, I managed two jobs while attending classes and commuting from West Kelowna. Despite the challenges, I was always punctual—often arriving 15 minutes before class started. This experience truly taught me that hard work pays off, and I am grateful to Elevare College for being part of my success.
I would highly recommend Elevare College to anyone looking for a supportive and positive learning environment.
