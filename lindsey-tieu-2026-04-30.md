I had such a great experience taking the Chairside Dental Assisting course at Ascentia Career Institute. The instructors are incredibly supportive and genuinely care about their student’s success. My instructor, Darcy, went above and beyond throughout my entire journey. Even after graduation, she continues to check in on me and is helping me with my job search, which means so much.

For my practicum, I was placed at Dr. Black’s office on Hardy Place, and it was an amazing experience. The whole team was welcoming, supportive, and always willing to share advice, not just for the practicum, but for starting my career afterward as well. They truly go above and beyond for their patients, and it was inspiring to be part of that environment. I always felt comfortable, encouraged, and valued while I was there.

I’m really grateful for everything I learned and the support I received. I highly recommend this program to anyone considering a career in dental assisting!
